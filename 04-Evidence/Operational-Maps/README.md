# Operational Maps

## Overview

Operational Maps provide visual representations of how information, authorization decisions, governance controls, recovery mechanisms, and operational activities move throughout the HyperSync Ecosystem.

While architecture documents describe system structure, Operational Maps illustrate system behavior.

Their purpose is to make ecosystem interactions understandable, observable, and reviewable.

---

## Architectural Principle

The Operational Map architecture is built around a simple principle:

> Complex systems become easier to govern when their behavior can be visualized.

Rather than relying solely on written documentation, Operational Maps provide visual representations of ecosystem activity, control flows, and governance pathways.

This improves transparency, communication, and operational understanding.

---

## Purpose of Operational Maps

Operational Maps help explain:

* How information moves
* How authorization decisions are made
* How governance controls are applied
* How operational systems interact
* How recovery mechanisms function
* How oversight is maintained

These visualizations provide a bridge between architecture and implementation.
