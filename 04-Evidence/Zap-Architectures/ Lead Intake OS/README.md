The Lead Intake Operating System (Lead Intake OS) serves as the entry layer of the HyperSync Lead Ecosystem.

Responsibilities include:

Lead ingestion
License validation
Data quality validation
Evergreen cleanup
Staging management
Lead qualification
External intake expansion
Audit logging

Workflow progression:

L-IN-Z1
Lead Intake Loader
        ↓
L-IN-Z2
TS Auto Delete Evergreen
        ↓
L-IN-Z2.1
SS Auto Delete Evergreen
        ↓
L-IN-Z3
External Intake Expansion

The Lead Intake OS acts as the controlled intake conveyor responsible for validating, staging, qualifying, cleaning, and expanding lead records before Process OS handoff.
