The Lead Outbound Operating System (Lead Outbound OS) serves as the CRM activation, campaign enrollment, engagement monitoring, and re-engagement layer of the HyperSync Lead Ecosystem.

Responsibilities include:

CRM onboarding
Campaign enrollment
Engagement signal processing
Intent classification
Lead nurturing
Re-engagement automation
CRM tagging
Audit logging

Workflow progression:

L-OUT-Z1
CRM Create / Update
        ↓
L-OUT-Z2
Campaign Enrollment Controller
        ↓
L-OUT-Z3
Engagement Signal Listener
        ↓
L-OUT-Z4
Re-engagement Scheduler

The Lead Outbound OS transforms validated leads into active CRM participants, tracks engagement signals, and automatically re-engages dormant opportunities.
