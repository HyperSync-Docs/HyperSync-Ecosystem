PRC-PZ3 — CRM Loader + Campaign Trigger

PRC-PZ3 serves as the CRM onboarding and campaign activation workflow of the Lead Process OS.

Responsibilities include:

Campaign trigger intake
Qualification validation
CRM contact creation
CRM contact updates
Contact ID extraction
Processing record creation
External webhook handoff
Success/Failure routing

The workflow transforms validated leads into active CRM records and campaign participants.
