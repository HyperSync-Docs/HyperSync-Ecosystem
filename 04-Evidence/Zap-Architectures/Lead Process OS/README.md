The Lead Process Operating System (Lead Process OS) serves as the validation, sanitization, routing, CRM preparation, and campaign activation layer of the HyperSync Lead Ecosystem.

Responsibilities include:

Data sanitization
Data normalization
Contact validation
Decision routing
CRM onboarding
Campaign activation
Audit logging
Outbound handoff preparation

Workflow progression:

PRC-PZ1
Data Sanitizer
        ↓
PRC-PZ2
Validator + Decision Router
        ↓
PRC-PZ3
CRM Loader + Campaign Trigger

The Lead Process OS transforms qualified intake records into validated CRM-ready contacts and prepares them for outbound campaign operations.
