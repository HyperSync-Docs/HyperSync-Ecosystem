The Lead System Operating System (Lead System OS) serves as the monitoring, audit, replay recovery, error handling, and operational alerting layer of the HyperSync Lead Ecosystem.

Responsibilities include:

Error logging
Audit tracking
Replay recovery
DeadLetter management
Operational alerting
Slack notifications
Email escalation
Compliance logging

Workflow progression:

L-SYS-SZ1
Lead Error Logger
        ↓
L-SYS-SZ2
Audit + Tracking
        ↓
L-SYS-SZ3
Lead Replay Controller
        ↓
L-SYS-S4
Ops Alerts

The Lead System OS provides observability, recovery, escalation, and governance controls across the Lead Ecosystem.
