Lend-OUT-Z1 — CRM Create/Update Borrower Client File

Lend-OUT-Z1 serves as the entry workflow of the Lender Outbound Operating System.

The workflow monitors outbound handoff records, validates readiness conditions, acquires processing ownership, synchronizes borrower information into HighLevel CRM, persists CRM results, and advances the outbound conveyor.

Responsibilities include:

Readiness validation
Processing lock acquisition
CRM contact creation/update
Borrower data synchronization
CRM evidence persistence
Outbound conveyor advancement

This workflow establishes the borrower CRM presence required for downstream campaign enrollment activities.
