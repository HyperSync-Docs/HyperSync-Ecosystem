Lend-OUT-Z2 — Campaign Enrollment Controller

Lend-OUT-Z2 serves as the borrower enrollment workflow of the Lender Outbound Operating System.

The workflow validates handoff readiness, acquires processing ownership, enrolls qualified borrowers into HighLevel campaign workflows, records enrollment evidence, and advances the outbound conveyor.

Responsibilities include:

Handoff readiness validation
Processing lock acquisition
Campaign enrollment
Workflow assignment
Enrollment evidence generation
Outbound conveyor advancement

The workflow ensures borrowers enter the correct campaign experience following CRM synchronization.
