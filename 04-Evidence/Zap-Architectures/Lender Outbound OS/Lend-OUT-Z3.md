Lend-OUT-Z3 — Loan Officer Assignment + Tasking

Lend-OUT-Z3 serves as the final workflow of the Lender Outbound Operating System.

The workflow validates outbound readiness, acquires ownership, records completion evidence, finalizes borrower lifecycle processing, and creates the System OS audit handoff record.

Responsibilities include:

Readiness validation
Ownership acquisition
Lifecycle completion tracking
Audit evidence generation
Replay protection
System OS handoff creation

This workflow completes the borrower outbound lifecycle and transfers ownership into the System Operating System.
