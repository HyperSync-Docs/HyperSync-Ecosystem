Lend-SYS-Z1 — Borrower Pipeline Audit Log

Lend-SYS-Z1 serves as the audit ingestion workflow of the Lender System Operating System.

The workflow receives completed borrower lifecycle handoffs from the Outbound Operating System, validates audit eligibility, constructs a standardized audit envelope, acquires audit ownership, and records audit evidence into governance repositories.

Responsibilities include:

Audit event validation
Audit envelope generation
Ownership acquisition
Audit record locking
Governance logging
Run-log generation

This workflow establishes the authoritative borrower audit trail used throughout the System OS.
