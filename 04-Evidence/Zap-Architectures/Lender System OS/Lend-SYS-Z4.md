Lend-SYS-Z4 — Performance + Funding Metrics Tracker

Lend-SYS-Z4 serves as the reporting and analytics workflow of the Lender System Operating System.

The workflow executes daily, aggregates operational data, calculates ecosystem metrics, and produces daily performance records.

Responsibilities include:

License validation
Alert aggregation
Validation aggregation
Funding aggregation
Metrics calculation
Daily performance reporting

The workflow generates daily operational intelligence for governance and performance monitoring.
