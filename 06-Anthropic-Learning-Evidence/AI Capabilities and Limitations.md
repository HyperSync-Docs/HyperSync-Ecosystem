# AI Capabilities and Limitations

## Overview

AI Capabilities and Limitations provides an introduction to how modern AI systems function, what they do well, where they can fail, and how humans should evaluate AI-generated outputs. The course emphasizes informed use, critical evaluation, and responsible human oversight.

## Learning Objectives

* Understand how AI systems operate
* Recognize AI strengths and limitations
* Evaluate AI-generated responses critically
* Apply human oversight to AI workflows
* Use AI responsibly in professional settings

## Skills Demonstrated

* AI Literacy
* Critical Evaluation
* Responsible AI Usage
* Human-in-the-Loop Decision Making
* Risk Awareness
* Ethical AI Collaboration

## HyperSync Alignment

Human oversight is a foundational principle throughout HyperSync. This course reinforces the repository's governance-first approach by emphasizing validation, auditability, and responsible AI collaboration rather than fully autonomous decision making.

## Repository Impact

Knowledge gained from this course supports:

* Governance Documentation
* Human Review Processes
* Compliance Architecture
* Risk Management
* Evidence-First Engineering
* Responsible Automation

AI Capabilities and Limitations strengthens the governance philosophy embedded throughout the HyperSync-Ecosystem and reinforces the importance of human accountability in AI-assisted systems.
