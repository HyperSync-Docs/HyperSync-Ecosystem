# AI Fluency for Builders

## Overview

AI Fluency for Builders introduces the mindset, technical principles, and implementation strategies required to design, build, and deploy AI-powered solutions. The course emphasizes practical development workflows, responsible engineering, iterative improvement, and collaboration between human expertise and artificial intelligence throughout the software development lifecycle.

## Learning Objectives

* Design AI-powered applications
* Apply AI throughout the development lifecycle
* Build scalable AI workflows
* Implement responsible engineering practices
* Improve solutions through iterative development
* Collaborate effectively with AI during software construction

## Skills Demonstrated

* AI Solution Development
* Engineering Workflows
* Iterative Design
* Responsible AI Engineering
* System Architecture
* AI-Assisted Development
