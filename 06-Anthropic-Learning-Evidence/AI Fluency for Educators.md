# AI Fluency for Educators

## Overview

AI Fluency for Educators prepares instructional leaders and educators to integrate AI responsibly into teaching, curriculum development, and institutional strategy. The course emphasizes ethical AI adoption, instructional design, and effective collaboration between educators and AI systems.

## Learning Objectives

* Develop AI literacy for educational environments
* Apply responsible AI practices
* Design AI-assisted learning experiences
* Evaluate AI tools critically
* Support institutional AI adoption
* Promote ethical AI collaboration

## Skills Demonstrated

* AI Literacy
* Responsible AI
* Instructional Design
* Educational Strategy
* Critical AI Evaluation
* Human-AI Collaboration
