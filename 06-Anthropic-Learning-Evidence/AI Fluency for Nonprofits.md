# AI Fluency for Nonprofits

## Overview

AI Fluency for Nonprofits explores how nonprofit organizations can responsibly adopt AI to improve operational efficiency, communication, fundraising, volunteer coordination, and mission impact. The course emphasizes ethical implementation, organizational governance, and practical AI adoption strategies.

## Learning Objectives

* Understand AI opportunities for nonprofit organizations
* Apply responsible AI governance
* Improve operational efficiency with AI
* Evaluate AI solutions ethically
* Support mission-driven decision making
* Develop sustainable AI adoption strategies

## Skills Demonstrated

* AI Governance
* Organizational Strategy
* Ethical AI Adoption
* Process Improvement
* Operational Planning
* Human-Centered AI
