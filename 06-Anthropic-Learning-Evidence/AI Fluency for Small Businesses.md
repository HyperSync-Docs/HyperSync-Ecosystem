# AI Fluency for Small Businesses

## Overview

AI Fluency for Small Businesses introduces practical strategies for adopting artificial intelligence within small business operations. The course focuses on identifying high-value use cases, improving operational efficiency, automating repetitive work, supporting business growth, and implementing AI responsibly while maintaining human oversight.

## Learning Objectives

* Identify practical AI opportunities for small businesses
* Improve operational efficiency through automation
* Evaluate AI solutions for business processes
* Implement responsible AI governance
* Increase productivity while maintaining quality
* Build sustainable AI adoption strategies

## Skills Demonstrated

* Business Process Automation
* Operational Efficiency
* AI Strategy
* Responsible AI Adoption
* Workflow Optimization
* Organizational Planning
