# AI Fluency for Students

## Overview

AI Fluency for Students prepares learners to use AI responsibly throughout their academic and professional development. The course emphasizes effective collaboration with AI systems, critical thinking, ethical decision-making, and the practical application of AI to learning and career planning.

## Learning Objectives

* Develop responsible AI collaboration skills
* Improve academic productivity using AI
* Evaluate AI-generated information critically
* Apply AI to career and professional development
* Understand ethical AI usage

## Skills Demonstrated

* AI Literacy
* Critical Thinking
* Responsible AI Usage
* Academic Productivity
* Human-AI Collaboration
* Professional Development
