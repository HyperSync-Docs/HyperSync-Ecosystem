# Introduction to Claude Cowork

## Overview

Introduction to Claude Cowork teaches how to collaborate with Claude across real projects, files, and research workflows. The course introduces the Cowork task loop, plugins, skills, and responsible AI collaboration techniques for managing multi-step work efficiently.

## Learning Objectives

* Understand the Claude Cowork workflow
* Collaborate with Claude on real-world projects
* Manage files and research using AI
* Execute multi-step task workflows
* Apply responsible AI collaboration practices

## Skills Demonstrated

* AI Collaboration
* Research Workflows
* File Management
* Multi-Step Task Planning
* Workflow Organization
* Productivity Optimization

## HyperSync Alignment

The Claude Cowork methodology closely mirrors the HyperSync development process. The structured collaboration model was applied throughout repository planning, GitHub artifact creation, phased implementation, and long-form engineering projects where AI serves as a collaborative development partner rather than a one-time assistant.
