# HyperSync Research Paper

## Evidence-Oriented Enterprise Automation Through Modular Operating Systems, Governance-Centered Architecture, and Human–AI Collaboration

HyperSync is a systems engineering research project investigating a governance-centered architecture for enterprise automation.

Unlike traditional workflow-centric automation platforms, HyperSync organizes enterprise operations into independently governed Operating Systems coordinated through standardized events, deterministic state management, integrated governance, and evidence-oriented engineering.

This repository accompanies the complete research manuscript and provides the architectural artifacts, implementation evidence, documentation, figures, and supplementary material supporting the publication.
