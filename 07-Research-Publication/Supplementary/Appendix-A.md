This appendix provides a consolidated mapping between the architectural concepts presented throughout the HyperSync manuscript and the implementation artifacts contained within the HyperSync Ecosystem repository.

# Evidence Philosophy

Every major architectural contribution presented throughout the HyperSync manuscript is supported by implementation artifacts preserved within the companion repository.

Rather than separating architecture from implementation, HyperSync adopts an evidence-oriented engineering methodology in which architectural concepts, implementation artifacts, governance documentation, operational demonstrations, and engineering evidence evolve together.

This methodology enables independent verification of architectural claims while improving reproducibility for future researchers.

# Research Value

The traceability model supports:

- Architectural verification
- Implementation reproducibility
- Governance transparency
- Reviewer comprehension
- Long-term research evolution
